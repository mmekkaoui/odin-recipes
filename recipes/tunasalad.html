Tuna Fish Salad

Description

Serve a scoop on top of a green salad, or between two pieces of bread with fresh lettuce. Sprinkle a little paprika on top to add a little flavor and color.

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • ¼ cup chopped celery
  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • paprika to taste

    • Steps

    1. Step 1

      2. In a large bowl, combine the tuna, celery, onion, mayonnaise, lemon juice, parsley, garlic powder, salt and pepper. Mix well and refrigerate until chilled. Sprinkle with paprika if desired.
